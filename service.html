    WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHY

    Wedding Photography is a speciality in photography that is primarily focused on the photography of events and relating to weddings .It may include other type if protrait photography of the couple before the official wedding day such as pre-wedding engagement session etc.

    PORTRAIT PHOTOGRAPHY

    Portrait photography is a very well-known and popular style of photography.This style of photographyis all about trying to capture the personality,identity,soul,and emotions of person by utilizing the background atmosphere,poses,and lighting.

    EVENT PHOTOGRAPHY

    Event photography is the professional art of snapping high-quality images during a wide variety of important occasions,from personal events like weddings or birthday parties to large public gathering like corporate events,galas,award ceremonies and music festivals.

    ENGAGEMENT PHOTOGRAPHY

    It is the portrait session taken place before the wedding that capture the newly engaged couple during this new and exciting time in their lives. An Engagement photography Session is usually scheduled shortly after finding the wedding photographers include one in their packages.

    I have always been fascinated by the beauty of human faces and in how many different ways it exists.Beauty is a wonderful genre of photography that include commercial and editorial imagery,as well as simple beauty portraits, so almost any professional or aspiring photographer who appreciates female beauty can find something in it for them.

    Corporate photography include all kinds of photographs used for a corporation's needs.The types of corporate photography include portrait,product photos,office environment photos,and event photography.
